The Little Prince

by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

philosophy fairytale from 0 to 100 y.o.

The simple tale tells the story of a child, the little prince, who travels the universe gaining wisdom. The novella has been translated into hundreds of languages and has sold some 200 million copies worldwide, making it one of the best-selling books in publishing history.
